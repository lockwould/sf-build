smartflower talks to you

Insights into your energy use, directly to your smartphone or tablet

smartflower is the first solar solution that communicates directly with you on a daily basis. As smartflower produces clean energy throughout the day, it automatically sends relevant data to the smartflower app, which you can access through any mobile device. The app gives you full visibility into the system’s production, your energy use and other valuable metrics to help you make smarter energy usage decisions.
