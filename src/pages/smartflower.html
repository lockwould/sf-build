Solar power just got smarter

Imagine if generating clean energy for your home or business were as easy as installing a refrigerator. That’s what smartflower has set out to do.

smartflower’s philosophy is to build environmentally sustainable energy solutions that are as easy to use as any other plug-and-play appliance. After a very short set up, smartflower is fully ready to start producing clean energy for you. And thanks to the array of built-in smart features, it’s completely autonomous and worry-free.

Advanced by design

  • Smart Tracking

    Autonomously follows the sun across two axes. Smartflower’s modular petals are always at the optimum angle for 40% more energy output than traditional solar.

  • Smart Cooling

    The system’s solar modules are always rear-ventilated, keeping it up to 18°F cooler to deliver 5–10% more output when compared to rooftop solar.

  • Smart Cleaning

    smartflower automatically cleans itself every time it folds and unfolds. Brushes on the back of each panel remove any contaminates leading to increased efficiency.

  • Smart Safety

    Smartflower is weatherproof and continuously monitors wind speeds, automatically folding itself into a secure position to prevent system damage.

  • Smart System

    The integrated control panel automatically sends you data on your energy use via the smartflower mobile app to help you decide how to better use your electricity.

  • Smart Use

    A fully integrated, plug-and-play solar solution without the complex installation. smartflower is as easy to disassemble as it is to set up, so you can take it anywhere.
