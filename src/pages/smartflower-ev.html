Solar power producer, electric vehicle charger, conversation starter

smartflower ev gives you all of the smart features and the stunning design of smartflower, and adds a charging station for electric vehicles.

The electricity generated by smartflower ev can be fed into the electric grid, or used directly when you charge an electric car or bike. smartflower ev can be installed almost anywhere in a small space with just an hour of setup time, making it perfect for residential use or commercially as an additional service for customers and the local community.

A high-performance solar system

With its 194 sq. ft. solar module fan, smartflower ev generates an average of between 3,400 and 5,700 kWh/yr* of power – up to 40% more than a roof-mounted system of comparable size. The electricity primarily is devoted to the s the integrated charging station, which is used to charge electric vehicles with a power supply of up to 22 kW. Any electricity not required for charging an e-vehicle is directed to the public grid.

*Production dependent on location

Advanced by design

  • Smart Tracking

    Autonomously follows the sun across two axes. Smartflower’s modular petals are always at the optimum angle for 40% more energy output than traditional solar.

  • Smart Cooling

    The system’s solar modules are always rear-ventilated, keeping it up to 18°F cooler to deliver 5–10% more output when compared to rooftop solar.

  • Smart Cleaning

    smartflower automatically cleans itself every time it folds and unfolds. Brushes on the back of each panel remove any contaminates leading to increased efficiency.

  • Smart Safety

    Smartflower is weatherproof and continuously monitors wind speeds, automatically folding itself into a secure position to prevent system damage.

  • Smart System

    The integrated control panel automatically sends you data on your energy use via the smartflower mobile app to help you decide how to better use your electricity.

  • Smart Use

    A fully integrated, plug-and-play solar solution without the complex installation. smartflower is as easy to disassemble as it is to set up, so you can take it anywhere.
